How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own timed range tests or tear down units in a lab. Instead, we compile recommendations by digging into years of owner feedback on Amazon, Reddit, and enthusiast forums, and by reading the most thorough expert reviews from outlets like OutdoorGearLab and Wirecutter. We pay close attention to what actually breaks, flimsy charging ports, finicky buttons, poor battery longevity, and prioritize models that hold up over the long haul. Our picks reflect consistent praise across multiple sources, with an honest look at trade-offs. No product is perfect; we highlight where a model might fall short (e.g., the RT22’s small speaker can sound tinny in noisy environments) so you can decide if the compromise matters for your use case. This approach gives you a researched, real-world perspective without pretending we’ve personally field-tested every unit.
